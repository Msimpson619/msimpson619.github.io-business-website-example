Our Story

Founded in 1985 by the Rossini family, Bella Vista has been serving authentic Italian cuisine for over three decades. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, combining traditional techniques with the finest ingredients.

Chef Marco Rossini, trained in Tuscany, brings his passion for Italian cooking to every dish. From our house-made pasta to our wood-fired pizzas, each meal is prepared with love and attention to detail.

We source our ingredients locally when possible and import specialty items directly from Italy to ensure the most authentic flavors in every bite.
